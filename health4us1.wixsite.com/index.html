    Health4US

    Dedicated to improving health literacy for the next generation 

    The Problem

    Healthcare is a fundamental human right. However, complicated medical terminology combined wit h a lack of proper education in the field has led to low health literacy rates across America. Because of this, widespread health inequities have emerged leading to disparities  amongst socioeconomic and demographic groups. 

    Services

    Our Work

    Through the A Child's Introduction to medical book series, we hope to expose grade school students to the health & medical field. Our books cover a vast array of topics the field and present introductory information in an engaging manner. Early education is crucial in order to improve health literacy as well as cultivate awareness and interest. 

     

     

    If you are interested in publishing your own A Child's Introduction to book with Health4US, fill out the form here. Health4US takes care of the publishing process, printing, and donates books to local libraries, schools, and museums.
